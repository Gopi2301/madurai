# ❌ The Problem

🍌 Food waste mixed with plastic  
🧃 Snack covers thrown anywhere  
⚠ Batteries & sharp waste ignored  
🧹 Clean workers struggle daily  

Kids are not taught:

❓ What is degradable?  
❓ What is non-degradable?  
❓ What is hazardous waste?  

No habit formation.  
No fun eco-learning system.

---

# 🌟 The Vision

## “Eco Education from Age 5.”

We teach children to understand:

🌱 **Degradable** – Goes back to nature  
🧴 **Non-Degradable** – Stays for years  
⚠ **Hazardous** – Harmful & needs special care  

Through:

📱 A gamified PWA  
🧠 AI-powered waste recognition  
🎤 Friendly Tamil audio guidance  
🏆 Reward-driven learning  

We build habits, not just awareness.

---

# 💡 The Solution

## How Green Madurai Works

1️⃣ 📸 Child uploads a waste photo  
2️⃣ 🧠 AI detects the category  
   - 🌱 Degradable  
   - 🧴 Non-Degradable  
   - ⚠ Hazardous  

3️⃣ 🎤 AI explains in natural kid-friendly Tamil  
4️⃣ 🗑 Suggests correct dustbin  
5️⃣ 🌟 Rewards points  
6️⃣ 👑 Updates leaderboard  

Learning → Action → Reward.
